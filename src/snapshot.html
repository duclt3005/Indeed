About us
ABOUT KROGER:
At Kroger, our people are our strength. With diverse backgrounds and unique experiences, they tell a story that is all ours – a story of friendliness, caring, teamwork and freshness. Together, we Feed the Human Spirit of millions of hungry people every single day. People who are hungry for more than just food. They’re hungry for something that can make their lives healthier, easier, brighter and a bit lighter. They’re hungry to feel good, do well, to go from anxiety to inspiration. So whether we manufacture, market, stock, check, deliver, or manage, our 450,000+ associates try to make their experience as uplifting as possible.
Kroger is redefining the way we shop for groceries by offering anything, anytime, anywhere, in our stores, at our pickup lanes or via our automated delivery vehicles. We aren’t just a traditional grocery store anymore, we are leading the way with the technology, talent, and rewards that provide every associate a career with promise. – -- more...
